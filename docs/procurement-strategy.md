# Tablet Procurement Strategy — No Distributor / No Credit

Cash-based procurement approach for sourcing refurbished and wholesale tablets without a distributor account or credit line.

**Context:** TD Synnex application was denied. D&H Distributing requires a credit check — not preferred. Investor backing available, enabling cash purchases.

---

## Primary Sources

### 1. B-Stock — Best Overall
Direct manufacturer and retailer liquidation auctions. No credit needed — cash bids only.

| Storefront | Link |
|------------|------|
| All tablet lots | https://bstock.com/auctions/computers/tablets/ |
| Apple iPad lots (direct from Apple) | https://bstock.com/auctions/consumer-electronics/apple-ipads/ |
| All auctions | https://bstock.com/all-auctions |

- Conditions range from Like New to salvage — maps directly to your Grade A / Grade B model
- Major brands run their own storefronts (Apple, Samsung, Amazon, mobile carriers)
- Free to register and browse before bidding
- **Tip:** Watch auctions for 1–2 weeks before bidding to learn pricing patterns

---

### 2. Tradeloop — Best for Specific Models
B2B marketplace for sourcing specific high-demand models rather than mixed pallets.

- [tradeloop.com/p/mobile/tablets](https://www.tradeloop.com/p/mobile/tablets?cat3id=1293)
- Verified wholesalers list bulk lots
- Good for targeting iPad Pro M4, Galaxy Tab S10, Surface Pro 11

---

### 3. BidAllies — Graded Bulk Lots
Pre-graded refurbished iPads and Android tablets, ready to resell.

- [bidallies.com/tablets.html](https://bidallies.com/tablets.html)
- Prepay, no distributor account required

---

### 4. Eze Wholesale
Accurately graded, ready-to-ship inventory. Prepay model.

- [ezewholesale.com/wholesale-tablets](https://www.ezewholesale.com/wholesale-tablets)

---

### 5. Corporate/Enterprise Lease Returns (Best Margins, Longer Play)
Companies refresh device fleets every 2–3 years and sell off-lease devices in bulk.

- **Liquid Technology** — buys and sells used enterprise tablets in bulk: [liquidtechnology.net/used-tablets](https://liquidtechnology.net/used-tablets/)
- **Tradeloop dealers** — many source from corporate fleets
- **Local ITAD companies** (IT Asset Disposal) — approach directly for recurring supply

---

## Why Cash Is an Advantage

B-Stock and similar platforms reward fast cash buyers. Credit-dependent buyers who need net terms are slower to close — cash buyers can win better lots at lower prices and move immediately when a good lot hits.

---

## Suggested Starting Approach

1. **Register on B-Stock** (free) — watch Apple iPad and Samsung tablet auctions for 1–2 weeks before bidding
2. **Set up a Tradeloop account** — use it to source specific high-demand models
3. **Start small** — begin with 10–20 unit lots to test grading quality before scaling up
4. **Revisit D&H or Ingram Micro** once monthly volume is established — distributor approvals get easier with a track record

---

## Distributor Path (Future)

Once volume is established, re-apply to:

| Distributor | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| **D&H Distributing** | More SMB-friendly, lower bar than TD Synnex. Requires credit check and resale certificate. Apply at [dandh.com](https://www.dandh.com/v4/view?pageReq=NSNewCust) |
| **Ingram Micro** | Full Apple/Samsung/Microsoft catalog, reseller API available. Apply at [ingrammicroresellerapplication.com](https://www.ingrammicroresellerapplication.com/) |
